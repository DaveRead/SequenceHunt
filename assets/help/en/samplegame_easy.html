Sequence Hunt - Sample Game

Difficulty: Easy

With difficulty set to easy the clues will indicate the color associated with the shape. This makes the determination of which colors are part of the solution straightforward.

Below is a depiction of a game along with an explanation of how to read the clues.

Note: in the descriptions each try is represented as having 4 positions. The positions are numbered from 1 to 4 as presented from left to right.

Try DepictionExplanation

The player enters yellow, red, blue and green, in that order. The clues report yellow and blue diamonds, a red triangle and a grayed X.

From this it is known that there is a yellow and blue at the guessed locations and that there is a red at a different location. There is no green.

Since the yellow and blue were correct, they are repeated in the same positions. The red was in the wrong position so it is guessed to be in the position 4. Black is guessed at position 2.

The clues report yellow, blue and red diamonds and a grayed X. The diamonds are not a surprise. The player has 3 of the positions correctly guessed. There is no black. Now the task is to figure out what color is in position 2.

The player keeps the three correct colors/position and guesses white for position 2. The player still gets the three diamonds for yellow, blue and red. So there is no white.

The player keeps the three correct colors and guesses yellow for position 2. The player still gets the three diamonds for yellow, blue and red. So there is no second yellow.

The player keeps the three correct colors and guesses red for position 2. The player still gets the three diamonds for yellow, blue and red. So there is no second red.

The player keeps the three correct colors and guesses blue for position 2.

This turns out to be the correct sequence!
